This map shows census demographic and income data for residents over the age of 60 years old. The demographic data is from the 2010 Census, while the income data is from the 2013 American Community Survey. Both datasets are at the block group level. Since the income and demographic data are from separate sources, the site does not allow for querying both income and demographic datasets at the same time. For more information about the Denver Regional Council of Governments Area Agency on Aging program visit the DRCOG Area Agency on Aging web-page
